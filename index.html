Overview

Crops are one of the main features of the game, serving as the main source of income. They're planted from seeds obtained from shops, seed packs, pets, or events. Once matured, they can be sold, gifted, or fed to pets. You can have up to 800 crops at once. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Types of Crops

Single Harvest Crops

These are fully collected on harvest—e.g., Bamboo, Mushroom, Boneboo. Use the Favorite Tool to avoid accidental collection. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Multi Harvest Crops

These regrow produce after harvest—e.g., Ember Lily, Mango, Dragon Fruit. Only the produce is collected. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Info

Seeds and Merchant

Seeds are available from Sam’s Shop, Tom’s Shop, Summer Seed Merchant, Georgia’s Shop, event shops like Easter 2025, Blood Moon, Twilight, Queen Bee, July 4th, Zen Shop, or the Crafting Station. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Crop Values

For specific Sheckle and Robux prices (e.g., Carrot → 10 Sheckles, 7 Robux; Beanstalk → 10M Sheckles, 715 Robux), refer to the original detailed page. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Trivia

  • The game once replaced Sam’s Shop with Tom’s in Update 1.11, then brought Sam back in 1.13. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
  • Obtainable crops are available through standard gameplay; Unobtainables originated from past events or testing and are no longer accessible. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
